◆ GDPR / Privacy

Privacy Policy

Luke Mathas Transfers collects only the information you choose to send when requesting transport, such as your name, phone number, production company, travel date, pickup and drop-off points, passenger count, luggage or equipment notes, and booking details. This information is used only to respond to your request, arrange transport, and communicate about the booking.

No analytics, advertising pixels, or non-essential tracking scripts are loaded on this website before consent. WhatsApp and phone links open only when you choose to click them. You may request correction or deletion of your booking information by contacting Loukas directly.

◆ Terms

Terms of Service

Transport is subject to availability, agreed schedule, passenger count, route, and production requirements. Quotes and bookings should be confirmed directly before the shoot day. Passengers are responsible for accurate pickup information, timing, and any special luggage or equipment notes.

This service is positioned for production transport, airport pickups, hotel-to-set transfers, and private group movement. It is not presented as a public taxi, tour-guide, or limousine service.
